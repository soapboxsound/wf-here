# WF-Here — Brand Bible

## The Brand in One Sentence
A map-based directory for finding great public workspaces — cafés, libraries, lobbies — with good wifi, food, and the right vibe. The anti-office.

## Name
**WF-Here** (styled lowercase as `wf—here`)
Domain: wf-here.com
The em dash (—) between wf and here is non-negotiable. It is part of the logo.

---

## Logo System

### Primary Mark
Classic red teardrop map pin + lowercase `wf—here` wordmark, side by side.
- Pin on left, wordmark on right
- 10px gap between pin and wordmark
- Pin vertically aligned to cap-height of wordmark
- Pin tip sits just below text baseline

### Secondary Mark
Same teardrop pin with `wf` typeset inside the round head.
- Used for: app icon, favicon, mobile nav, social profiles, stickers
- `wf` in SF Pro Display Medium (500), white (#F8F8F6)
- Text centered on the circle portion of the pin (not the full bounding box)

### Files
- `logo-primary.svg` — pin + wordmark, light backgrounds
- `logo-primary-dark.svg` — pin + wordmark, dark backgrounds
- `logo-secondary.svg` — icon mark only
- `favicon.png` — 32×32px, red pin shape only
- `app-icon.png` — 512×512px, secondary mark

## Voice & Tone

**Personality:** Witty, warm, insider.
Talks like a friend who always knows the best spot.

**Sounds like:**
- "Good wifi. No small talk."
- "Your office called. You didn't pick up."
- "Curated by people who actually work there."
- "Know a spot? Add it. Someone's looking for it right now."
- "If it passes the vibe check, it goes on the map."

**Never sounds like:**
- Corporate or hustle-coded
- Overly enthusiastic or startup-jargon
- A productivity app
- A coffee shop loyalty program

---

## Brand Rules

1. The em dash in `wf—here` is always an em dash (—), never a hyphen (-).
2. Pin red (#E8281A) is the only accent color. Never use it for anything other than pins and primary CTAs.
3. Featured business pins are black (#111111), not red. This distinction is sacred.
4. The wordmark is always lowercase: `wf—here`. Never `WF-Here` in the logo itself.
5. System font only — never load a web font for the wordmark.
6. No gradients, no shadows on the logo, no decorative frames.
7. Minimum clear space around the logo = height of the pin on all sides.
