Working in Los Angeles

Working in Los Angeles means accepting that your office will change depending on where you are that day. The city rewards flexibility. A meeting in Culver City, a focus block in Silver Lake, a late afternoon in Venice — each neighbourhood has its version of the right spot.

The key variables in LA are parking, wifi reliability, and noise. Hotel lobbies in DTLA and Santa Monica are underused gold mines. The café culture in Silver Lake and Los Feliz is strong but competitive — arrive before 9am or accept that you are getting the table by the bathroom. Venice has the best combination of outdoor seating and fast wifi of any neighbourhood in the city.
